Samgyupsal


Ingredients

  • 14-16 green onions (about 5 cups), sliced thinly
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoon hot pepper flakes (gochugaru)
  1. Heat up your grill plate or pan. Put a little toasted sesame oil on the hot plate for flavor.
  2. Cover the grill plate with pork belly. Grill them and turn them over when the bottoms get a little crispy. Keep cooking and turning them over until both sides are crispy.
  3. As the pieces get ready to eat, take them off the grill and put them on a small side plate. People can take the pieces from this plate, or if theyâ€™re too hungry they can take them right off the grill!
  4. Eat, but keep cooking!
